About Us

Welcome to the Immersive Interaction Lab at Grand Valley State University.

We aim to merge digital and physical information in ways that preserves the strenghts of both mediums. We develop and study enabling technologies, which make information more accessible, primarily through Mixed Reality interfaces. Our research defines how future immersive systems should be developed for use in both general and sepcilized settings.

The Immersive Interaction Lab is led by Dr. Talha Khan. If you want to work with us, please reach out to him.
